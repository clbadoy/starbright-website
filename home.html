Physical, Occupational, and Speech Therapy

Physical, Occupational and Speech therapists are specially trained to assist our patients in improving their abilities in recovery and performing daily functions.

With comprehensive rehabilitation services, our professionals can aid patients with their movement, mobility, independence, speech and other factors related to daily activities.

Multilingual Assistance with Warm & Respect

With a rich diversity of ethnicities and nationalities, we entertain an unsurpassed environment of friendliness and mutual respect.

We also proudly accommodate the multicultural nature of the Southern California area.

Medical Social Services and Certified Home Health Aide

Social workers provide counseling to patients and their loved ones to help them cope with social and emotional aspects related to the specific illness.

Our home health aide assists in activities of daily living and personal care (meal preparation, walking, bathing, toileting, and dressing) for patients.

Our Services

Clinical Supervision

Ongoing oversight, guidance, and support provided by experienced clinicians to ensure quality patient care, staff competency, and compliance with healthcare standards at home.

Medication Supervision

Overseeing and assisting patients to ensure medications are taken correctly, safely, and as prescribed.

Health Counseling

Guiding and educating individuals to make informed decisions and adopt behaviors that promote their overall health and well-being.

Blood Draw & Lab Work

Collecting blood samples and conducting laboratory tests to assess a patient’s health, diagnose conditions, or monitor treatment progress.

Wound Care

Assessment, cleaning, treatment, and monitoring of wounds to promote healing, prevent infection, and reduce complications.

Intravenous Drip (I.V.)

Administration of fluids, medications, or nutrients directly into a vein in controlled amounts over a set period of time.
